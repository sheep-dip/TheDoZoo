PLAY NOW!

A fun new way to get stuff done! The DoZoo is a game you can play in your browser. In The DoZoo, you can raise your very own virtual pet from an egg and nurture it while doing something productive! The DoZoo is full of fun and unique creatures (DoZees) that have distinct designs and personalities. The DoZoo will always be free to play and family friendly. Let’s get productive!

“A new browser game?”, You may be asking. “Yes!”, I may be replying. Unfortunately, browser games aren’t as popular as they were a decade or two ago. You’ve got me as to why. I loved playing these sorts of games growing up. Free, no downloads, endless possibilities, playable on any device that had an internet browser and decent html support. It was perfect! And don’t say they were replaced by mobile games. If I wanted to dedicate 500 megabytes to a money-munching time waster that demands daily actions in exchange for formulaic, mediocre gameplay, then I would just move back to the family farm!

The DoZoo may not be brilliant enough to entirely revitalize browser gaming, but I’m honored to claim my place as one of the last bastions of this beloved genre. But I wouldn’t be a good business boy if I didn’t have a gimmick in mind when making the game. The cool thing about The DoZoo is that it’s not just a browser game. It’s also a life-saving tool! So much time is wasted these days on idle hours. Have you ever taken a “quick peek” at your preferred content feed that ended up eating multiple hours of your day? You had homework to do! Your family had dinner without you! You could have been working on your novel! You didn’t even notice that your car has been stolen by your daughter’s ex-boyfriend and he’s just been spotted cruising down the I-18 going 38 miles over the speed limit with the fuzz hot on his trail and if he gets one scratch on Matilda then prison will be a pizza party compared to what you’ll do to him when he gets out!

What was I even talking about? Oh, yeah. The DoZoo! Procrastination is a pain! My friend* Dr. Wilbur told me four things, “People tend to be more productive when they’re waiting for something.”, “People are capable of getting more done in 20 minutes than they often realize.”, “Get out of my car. I already told you I don’t have any more soft-boiled eggs.”, and “No, I don’t have any hard-boiled either. You ate those ones before you ate the soft-boiled.” The DoZoo might be just what you need to get stuff done like you’ve never believed you could before! It’s also a fresh and groovy browser game. I mean, have you seen the characters up there? Look how cute they are!

*imaginary friend

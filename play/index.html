Browser game — The DoZoo — Version 1.0 — Created and developed by Aidan Byrd

Directions: Hatch your egg by clicking and waiting to see which DoZee will be born! Then, name and nurture your new pet. Go get things done while you wait for cooldowns to finish.

Controls: Move mouse, point, and click OR use touchscreen on supported devices.

Tips:

  • If the game window isn’t visible on your screen, the timer isn’t going down. This way, you can’t use other apps if you’re playing on your phone. You’re welcome.
  • The hunger and thirst meters are always depleting. Even while the timer is going. As soon as they hit zero, it’s game over, so don’t forget to check up on your pet.
  • There’s no way to save your game. See how much progress you can get with your DoZee before you have to close your tab. Feel free to take a screenshot if you want to show off your progress.
  • If your DoZee’s happiness reaches MAX, then it will evolve into a new, bigger DoZee. This is not possible quite yet, because I need to update the game, and a DoZee isn’t truly happy until I’ve allowed them to be.
  • Don’t worry about your virtual pet’s well-being when you close the tab. They aren’t dead. They just leave the virtual world and enter our reality. Every abandoned DoZee is spawned into a human body when it enters our world. Whenever you meet someone new, it could be a former DoZee. Your best friend could be a DoZee that only became friends with you to exact revenge for abandonment when the time is right. DoZee-turned humans are also fully capable of reproducing naturally, so your parents could even be DoZees
  • Don’t forget to have fun!
